A lovely image of a chicken burger.

The all famous chicken burger. Something of a spectacular nature, but also comforting... with a few adjustments, we can make it wild and fiery, or sweet and calm. The temperment of childhood joy, or the companion of a hard worker, most can agree, the chicken burger is something to rival all other foods. Quite lovely.

Ingredients

  • 1 x Crumbed chicken patty
  • 1 x medium sized avocado
  • 1 x Onion
  • 1 x Tomato
  • 1 x Bunz
  • 1 x Sauce

Steps

  1. Fry patty on medium heat for 16 minutes.
  2. Prep onions, tomatoes and avocado.
  3. Butter buns, and fry on buttered side.
  4. Add fried patty.
  5. Add choppings.
  6. Voila! Enjoy!
